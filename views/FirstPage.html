What is ThoughtSpot?

ThoughtSpot is an online journal designed to help you develop your mindfulness and introspection through documenting your daily life into one easy to use online journal. ThoughtSpot contains multiple features that make it easier to keep track of how you feel each day, such as a mood meter. It also keeps track of the time and date for you!
